Gravitational Force Fundamentals (15 min)

Definition (Gravitational Force The gravitational force:).
  • Is always attractive
  • Acts along the line connecting centers of mass
  • Can be considered to act at the center of mass of objects
  • Has infinite range (affects all objects in universe)
  • Is proportional to both masses
  • Is inversely proportional to the square of the distance between centers

Common Misconceptions (10 min)

Do astronauts experience "zero gravity" in orbit?
  • Astronauts are still affected by Earth's gravity
  • They experience "weightlessness" because they are in free fall (falling around Earth)
  • Both the astronaut and spacecraft fall at the same rate
Is gravitational force the same as weight?
  • Weight is the specific case of gravitational force between an object and Earth
  • Gravitational force is more general (between any two masses)
