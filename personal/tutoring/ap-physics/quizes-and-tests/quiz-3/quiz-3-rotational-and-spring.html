Question 1

A uniform rod of length L and mass M is pivoted at one end and released from rest in a horizontal position. What is the magnitude of the angular acceleration of the rod immediately after release?

A) g/L
B) 2g/L
C) 3g/L
D) 3g/2L

Question 2

A spring with spring constant k is compressed by distance x from its equilibrium position. Which of the following expressions gives the elastic potential energy stored in the spring?

A) kx
B) kx²
C) kx²
D) 2kx²

Question 3

A small object of mass m is attached to a string of length r and swung in a horizontal circle on a frictionless surface. The string makes an angle θ with the vertical as shown in the diagram. What provides the centripetal force needed for this circular motion?

A) A component of the tension in the string
B) A component of the gravitational force
C) The normal force from the surface
D) The resultant of the tension and gravitational forces

Question 4

A student sits on a frictionless rotating stool holding two 2.0 kg masses. Initially, the masses are extended 0.90 m from the axis of rotation, and the student is rotating with an angular velocity of 0.75 rad/s. If the student pulls the masses in to a distance of 0.30 m from the axis of rotation, what physical quantity is conserved in this process?

A) Angular velocity
B) Rotational kinetic energy
C) Angular momentum
D) Moment of inertia

Question 5

A 0.50 kg mass attached to a spring with spring constant 200 N/m oscillates with an amplitude of 0.10 m. What is the maximum speed of the mass during the oscillation?

A) 2.0 m/s
B) 0.50 m/s
C) 4.0 m/s
D) 20 m/s

Question 6

A uniform disk and a uniform hoop, both with the same mass and radius, are released from rest at the top of an inclined plane. Assuming both objects roll without slipping, which one reaches the bottom first and why?

A) The disk, because it has a smaller moment of inertia
B) The hoop, because it has a larger moment of inertia
C) Both reach the bottom at the same time, because their masses and radii are the same
D) The result depends on the angle of the incline

Question 7

A 2.0 kg block attached to a horizontal spring with spring constant 50 N/m oscillates on a frictionless surface. If the block's maximum displacement from equilibrium is 0.20 m, what is the mechanical energy of the system?

A) 0.50 J
B) 1.0 J
C) 2.0 J
D) 4.0 J

Question 8

An object of mass m is attached to a vertical spring with spring constant k and allowed to come to equilibrium. If the spring stretches by distance d from its unstretched length, which of the following expressions gives the spring constant?

A) k = m/d
B) k = mg/d
C) k = md/g
D) k = g/md
