Debugging Procedure 1:

We're not getting a simple boolean result, the signal is not close to dark count levels on horizontal, or vertical

To debug,

  • we're going to go through each gate in our experimental setup starting with the furthest right HWP,
  • calculate what the fit equation should be if we vary that gate alone, and
  • if experimentally fitting that gate matches,
    • the gate itself was the problem.
  • If it doesn't,
    • it's a subsequent gate as the issue.
