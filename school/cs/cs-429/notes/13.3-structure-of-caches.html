Cache Accessing

We do run into a #pigeonhole* problem, so how do we identify a single pigeon from multiple pigeons in the same hole? With hash-maps we solved this with linked lists.
We want a constant time algorithm to access our cache. The size of our hashmap, of our reduced cache domain, is called the "cache set"
